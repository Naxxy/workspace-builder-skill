## Process

Read `output/research-question.md`.

Identify and annotate sources relevant to the research question. Aim for 8–15 sources. Prioritise coverage of different perspectives and methodologies over confirmation of a single view.

For each source produce:
- Full citation (author, title, publication, year)
- 2–3 sentence summary of the source's main claim or contribution relevant to the question
- Key data points, findings, or arguments directly applicable to the question
- A brief reliability note: methodology strength, sample size, recency, peer-review status

Do not compare sources or begin synthesising at this stage. Record what each source says; the extract stage handles comparison.
